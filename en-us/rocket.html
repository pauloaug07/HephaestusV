Design

We opted for a classic cone-shaped-tip long-body rocket which is ideal for our current scale.

Measurements:

  • Tip length: 15cm;
  • Body length: 1m;
  • Fin length (4): 10cm.

Added length: 1.55m.

Considering the materais we're planning on using, it should be relatively light and inexpensive to manufacture.

Materials

As mentioned earlier, our goal is to provide a cheaper, greener alternative to small-scale rocketing.

We're aiming to build the rocket with the following materials:

  • Cardboard;
  • Plastic.
